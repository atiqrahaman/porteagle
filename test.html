About Us

Port Eagle focus on only one department which is Import to Bangladesh. Port Eagle is a regional specialist handling all Intra–Asia containerized & Air cargo. Port Eagle represents a group of service oriented and knowledgeable people providing customers with a wide-range direct port coverage and a large corridor portfolio in the Intra Asian market. We understand the requirements and dynamics of the Intra-Asian trade. We are flexible in our operations but at the same time maintain a strong focus on schedule reliability.

Our Services

Sea & Import Forwarding

  • Import from Intra Asia (China, Hong Kong, India, Vietnam, Singapore) to Bangladesh through various carrier/Airlines as per client requirement.

Custom Clearance

  • Port Eagle does custom clearance at Chittagong port, Dhaka Airport & ICD Dhaka for both Export & import cargo.

Door to Door Import

  • Import Logistics (China to Dhaka). We receive cargo at our warehouse in China & deliver to customer at Dhaka. Customers don’t need to handle LC/TT, Freight forwarder, C&F, Local transportation.
